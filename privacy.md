**Privacy Policy for Sakura Chat**

**Last updated:** November 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sakura Chat ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

**Information We Collect**

We may collect and store information you provide directly to us, including:

* **Account Information:** When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we receive basic profile information from Google, such as your email address and name, to create and manage your account.
* **User Profile Data:** To personalize your learning experience, we may ask for and store information you provide, such as your name, profession, learning goals, and interests. This data is stored securely on your device.
* **Conversation Data:** We store your chat history locally on your device to allow you to review past conversations.
* **Online AI Chat Data:** When you use the "Online" AI mode, your messages (and relevant profile data for context) are sent to Google's Generative AI (Gemini) service to generate a response. We do not store these conversations on our servers.

**How We Use Your Information**

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
* Authenticate your account using Firebase and Google Sign-In.
* Personalize your chat experience by tailoring AI responses to your learning goals and profile.
* Respond to your support requests.

**Data Storage**

Your account information and chat history are stored securely in a local SQL database on your device. This data is not automatically uploaded to our servers and remains private to you.

**Third-Party Services**

We use the following third-party services:

* **Firebase Authentication (Google):** To handle user sign-in securely.
* **Google Generative AI (Gemini):** To power the online AI chat responses. Your interactions with the AI are subject to Google's privacy policies.

**Children's Privacy**

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

**Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
